```tsx
    <div className="prose">
      {/* Chapter I */}
      <h2>ГЛАВА І</h2>
      <div className="article-box">...</div>
      
      {/* Chapter II */}
      <h2>ГЛАВА ІІ</h2>
      <div className="article-box">...</div>
      
      {/* ... Chapters III-VII ... */}
      
      {/* Download Button */}
      <Button onClick={downloadPDF}>
        Read Official PDF
      </Button>
    </div>
  </CardContent>
</Card>
```

### **Styling Classes:**
- `prose prose-slate dark:prose-invert` - Typography
- `article-box` - Custom article container
- `border-l-4 border-primary` - Left accent border
- `bg-muted/20 hover:bg-muted/30` - Interactive background
- `font-bold text-primary` - Point numbers styling

### **Search Integration:**
- All content wrapped in `#ustav-content`
- Search bar highlights matches
